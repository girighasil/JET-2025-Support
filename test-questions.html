Sample Test Questions

Question: What is the capital of France?

Type: mcq

Points: 2

A) Berlin

B) *Paris

C) London

D) Madrid

Explanation: Paris is the capital and most populous city of France.

Question: Which of the following is a prime number?

Type: mcq

A) 4

B) 6

C) *7

D) 8

Points: 1

Negative Points: 0.5

Explanation: A prime number has exactly two factors: 1 and itself. 7 is only divisible by 1 and 7.

Question: Fill in the blank: The process of plants making their own food using sunlight is called ______.

Type: fillblank

Answer: photosynthesis

Points: 2

Explanation: Photosynthesis is the process by which green plants and some other organisms use sunlight to synthesize foods with carbon dioxide and water.

Question: Explain the concept of gravity in your own words.

Type: subjective

Keywords: force, attraction, mass, Newton, pull

Points: 5

Explanation: A good answer should explain gravity as the force of attraction between masses, reference Newton's law of universal gravitation, and explain how gravity affects objects on Earth.
